Setup your calendar with Nextcloud on Arch Linux

Thu, Apr 5, 2018

Ever wanted to get rid of your dependency on Google Calendar?

Install Nextcloud by following the instruction here.

Install the nextcloud-app-calendar package:

pacman -S nextcloud-app-calendar

Login as admin on your nextcloud instance, go to settings > applications > disabled and enable the Calendar app.
